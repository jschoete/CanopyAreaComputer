With the aid of the algorithm described in Timothy Shields' answer, do:
1) compute area of (interesting) inner circles, say A1
2) compute area of (interesting) inner circles AND outer circle, say A2
3) outer area = A2 - A1
4) inner area = area of outer circle - outer area
